Trust

This product should only go public if it stays boring in the right ways.

  • Source-first job facts only.
  • No login-gated or LinkedIn scraping.
  • No mass-copied full job descriptions.
  • Generated summaries must add value without inventing facts.
  • Freshness and removal posture matter as much as discovery volume.
  • No user accounts, CV uploads, or candidate profiling in the first public release.
  • Direct apply should go to the employer or official source, not through a maze of partner links.
Trust promise: show where the job came from, when it was last checked, and what the site added beyond the original listing.
