Terms

These terms describe the public-beta posture of Hubsays Live + Work Map. This site is an informational, source-first navigation layer for relocation and job discovery. It is not a recruitment agency, job-placement service, immigration advisor, or financial adviser.

Service posture

  • Hubsays provides summaries, comparison tools, and links back to original sources.
  • Users should always verify the current role, salary, visa, and application details at the original source.
  • Hubsays does not host applications, candidate messaging, recruiter inboxes, or resume storage in this beta.

No professional advice

  • Job, salary, tax, visa, language, and relocation signals are informational only.
  • Nothing on this site is legal, immigration, tax, employment, or financial advice.
  • If a decision is high-stakes, users should confirm it with the original source or a qualified professional.

Corrections and removals
