Transparency

Hubsays Jobs Map should work as a human-reviewed, AI-assisted navigation layer for international job seekers. It should not behave like a black-box recruiter or a resume warehouse.

  1. Jobs come from official career pages, approved APIs, or permitted feeds.
  2. Automation normalizes the listing, tags language and visa posture, and drafts a short expat snapshot.
  3. Human review stays responsible for publication posture and risky claims.
  4. Public apply actions should send the user straight to the employer or official source.
Visa likely is not visa guaranteed. Dutch sponsor and immigration signals can improve discovery, but final eligibility always depends on the employer and the authorities.
AI use: AI may assist with summaries, language tags, and fit notes. It should not invent salary, legal, immigration, or hiring guarantees.
