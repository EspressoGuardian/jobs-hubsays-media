Privacy

This private preview runs locally with no ads, no analytics, and no affiliate scripts loaded.

Public launch is intended to stay tracker-light. Ads or optional analytics should remain behind reviewed consent wiring.

  • No user accounts in v1.
  • No resume uploads or candidate profiles in v1.
  • No search-history warehouse for individual users.
  • Only minimal operational logging should remain once public.
Lowest-risk launch path is a browse-and-click product with direct employer apply links and no personal-data intake.
