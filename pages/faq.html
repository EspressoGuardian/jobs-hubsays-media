Frequently Asked Questions

Does a higher score mean I should definitely move there?

No. The score is a structured trade-off summary, not legal, tax, or immigration advice.

Do you track users or build personal profiles?

No in this beta. The decision-desk sliders are stored locally in the browser only.

How often is the data updated?

The target posture is a governed static refresh cycle with explicit versioning, not silent live scraping.

Does a hiring signal guarantee that a role is still open?

No. Hiring signals are freshness clues, not guarantees. Always verify the current role at the original source.

Why are there no housing listings yet?

Because the safer first step is city-level affordability and rent-pressure signals, not risky listing ingestion.
