Data Sources

Target public-source posture:

  • Official company career pages
  • Structured ATS endpoints such as Greenhouse, Lever, Workable, Recruitee, and Teamtailor where allowed
  • Approved partner APIs and feeds
  • Dutch sponsor and public-registry enrichment where it adds a real factual signal
  • Permitted public sources reviewed case by case

Not in scope:

  • LinkedIn scraping
  • Large-scale copied job-description text
  • Recruiter names, direct emails, or other personal data stored as product content
  • Republished full news articles or large snippets
The safest posture is to store structured metadata plus a short summary and then link out to the original source.
