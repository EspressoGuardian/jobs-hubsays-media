About

Hubsays Jobs Map is a private preview for a map-led jobs product aimed at English-speaking, expat-friendly, and language-specific job discovery in Europe.

The preview is local-only. It exists to validate the interface, taxonomy, trust posture, and monetization path before any public launch.

Product posture: this should launch as a navigation layer for internationals, not as a high-risk job board with candidate accounts, CV storage, or opaque ranking.
  • Direct-source and direct-apply posture first.
  • Metadata plus short expat snapshots instead of copied full listings.
  • Human-in-the-loop review for AI-generated summaries and risk signals.
  • Privacy-first MVP: no accounts, no resumes, no hidden tracking.
