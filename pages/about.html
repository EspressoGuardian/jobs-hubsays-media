About

Hubsays Live + Work Map is a public beta for relocation-aware job discovery aimed at internationals comparing where they can realistically live and work in Europe.

The product is designed as a decision-support layer, not a recruitment agency. It combines job metadata, city signals, and relocation context to help users evaluate practical move options.

Product posture: source-first metadata, short human-reviewed summaries, and no candidate accounts or CV storage in the first public release.
  • Direct-source and direct-apply posture first.
  • Metadata plus short expat snapshots instead of copied full listings.
  • Human-in-the-loop review for AI-generated summaries and risk signals.
  • Privacy-first MVP: no accounts, no resumes, no hidden tracking.
