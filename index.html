Zero-data by default

No account system, no candidate database, no CV vault, and no on-site application pipeline in this beta.

Source-first routing

Every role should resolve to the employer or official source. Hubsays is the navigation layer, not the destination.

Decision support only

Salary, rent, visa, and language signals are directional heuristics for faster decisions, not guarantees or legal advice.

Job information originates from third-party sources. Hubsays does not host applications, verify every listing, or promise outcomes. Use the compare tools to decide where to focus, then apply externally.

How This Beta Works

  • No sign-in, resume uploads, saved-job backend, or email capture in this beta.
  • Roles are shown as structured metadata and short summaries, not copied full job descriptions.
  • Apply actions should always send users to the employer or official source.
  • Visa, language, and hiring-signal fields are decision support only, never guarantees.
  • Correction and removal requests should stay visible and easy to use.
